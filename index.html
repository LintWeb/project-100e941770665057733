About Hatekhori

Hatekhori is more than just a school; it's a foundation for lifelong learning. Established with the vision of providing holistic education, we cater to students from Grade 1 through Grade 8, ensuring a seamless transition through the formative years of childhood development.

Experienced Faculty

Our teachers are dedicated professionals committed to individual student growth.

Modern Curriculum

Blending traditional values with modern pedagogical approaches for a balanced education.

Safe Environment

A secure and nurturing campus designed for safe learning and play.

Our Educational Programs

Comprehensive learning pathways for every stage of development.

Primary Years (1-5)

Focusing on core literacy, numeracy, and social skills in a creative environment. We build the base for critical thinking and inquiry.

Middle School (6-8)

Transitioning to deeper academic disciplines, self-discovery, and leadership skills to prepare students for high school challenges.

Extracurriculars

From arts and music to physical education, we ensure every child finds their passion beyond the classroom.
